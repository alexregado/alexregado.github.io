Lush Modern Classic Residence in Dubai

Covet House x Vyde Studio

Situated in the epicenter of the "City of Gold", is a lavish project designed by Vyde Studio featuring some of the most iconic furniture, lighting, upholstery, and accessory pieces from Covet House. A residence in Dubai, with a very sleek and appealing modern classic aesthetic, where every element tells an intriguing story. Resembling and evokes a classic feel with the use of marbles, boiserie, and carved statues, while the furniture and decoration are very reminiscent of a contemporary style.
